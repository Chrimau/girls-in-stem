    About Us

    We support women in STEM fields through mentorship and resources.


    Girls in Stem is a project of UOF, a non-profit organization that aims to increase the number of women and girls in STEM fields. We believe that by promoting diversity and inclusion, we can create a more inclusive and successful future for all.


    Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment for girls to explore their interests and pursue their passions in STEM fields. We are dedicated to creating an inclusive environment where girls can thrive and succeed.


    We believe that by fostering a supportive and inclusive environment, we can create a more diverse and inclusive society. We are committed to providing resources and support to girls in STEM fields, including mentorship, guidance, and opportunities for learning and growth.

    Impact Stories

    Sarah Onwunatujo

    Meet Sarah, a passionate young woman who always dreamed of becoming an engineer. Growing up in a small town, she faced many challenges, including limited access to resources and mentorship.

    In high school, Sarah joined our NGO’s mentorship program, where she was paired with a professional engineer who provided guidance, support, and encouragement. Through workshops and networking events, Sarah gained the skills and confidence she needed to pursue her dreams.
    After completing the program, Sarah applied to several engineering colleges and was accepted into her top choice! Today, she is thriving in her studies, actively participating in student organizations, and inspiring her peers.

    Sarah’s story is just one example of how our program is changing lives and empowering women to excel in STEM fields. With continued support, we believe she will make a significant impact in the engineering world.

    Unit Conversions
    Temperature



    To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `c` with your temperature in celsius: (c x 9/5) + 32
    Weight




    To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.205
    Distance



    To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.609
